Shree-Nath Sweets.

Founded in 2001, Shree-Nath Sweets Pvt. Ltd. has been a beacon of excellence in the food industry, proudly rooted in the vibrant state of Gujarat, India. Our inaugural retail outlet, located at Stadium Circle, Navrangpura, marked the beginning of a journey that has since flourished into a thriving network of over fifty outlets. These are strategically situated in prime business and residential hub in Kalol.
Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Dinesh Yadav (Chairman), the Shree-Nath group has expanded its horizons to encompass a diverse array of culinary experiences


Mission and Vision.

At Shree-Nath Sweets, we strive to set the standard for quality, service, and hygiene across our sweet shops, restaurants, and catering services. Our mission is to provide exceptional customer satisfaction by maintaining consistent product excellence and delivering outstanding service, ensuring every interaction with us is filled with delight and trust.
